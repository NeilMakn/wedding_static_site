The Couple

Legend says that Kendra and Neilson met in kindergarden. Apparently Kendra laughed at Neilson when he got sent to time-out for saying "butt". Kendra doesn't remember this, she was too busy drawing on herself with markers. Fast forward to high school: Neilson swore less and Kendra decided to switch to drawing with pens. They met in English class junior year, and Kendra was attracted to Neilson's puns and silly songs. Neilson was drawn to Kendra's sweet demeanor, smarts, and good looks. Their flirtation caused them to get in trouble in history class, but culminated in prom, dates, graduation, moving to another city, and developing a deep sense of companionship, trust, and love.
